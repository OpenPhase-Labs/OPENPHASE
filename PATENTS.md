## 2. The "Peace Treaty" (Retaliation Clause)

This patent grant is conditioned on your continued cooperation with the open
ecosystem. Under Section 5.2 of the MPL 2.0, if you initiate patent litigation
against any entity alleging that this protocol or its implementation infringes
your patents, your rights to use this software and these patents will
terminate immediately.

## 3. Scope of the Pledge

* **Open Extensions:** Any modifications or extensions to the core protocol files
  (the `.proto` files) must remain open under the MPL 2.0.
* **Proprietary Use:** You are free to build proprietary applications (e.g.,
  traffic controller software, ATMS platforms, analytics dashboards) that use
  this protocol without being forced to open-source your private application
  logic, provided you do not modify the licensed protocol files themselves in
  a closed manner.

## 4. Goal

The intent of this licensing structure is to ensure that OpenPhase remains
a shared, extensible, and versionable standard for the traffic signal industry,
preventing any single vendor from creating a proprietary fork that closes the
ecosystem.
